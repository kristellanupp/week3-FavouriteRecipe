Red Velvet Cake Recipe

INGREDIENTS

Dry Ingredients

  • 2 2/3 cups (400 g) plain cake flour
  • 2 tbsp (10 g) cocoa powder, unsweetened
  • 1 tsp (5 g) baking soda / bi-carb soda
  • Pinch of salt

Wet Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (115 g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups (330 g) caster / superfine white sugar
  • 2 eggs , at room temperature
  • 1 cup (250ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract (or essence)
  • 1 cup (250 ml) buttermilk, at room temperature
  • 2 1/2 tbsp red food colouring liquid

Frosting

  • 14 oz (400 g) Philadelphia Cream Cheese, block
  • 1/2 cup (115 g) unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 cups (450 g) soft icing sugar / powdered sugar

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 180C/350F (all oven types). Butter 2 x 21cm / 8″ round cake pans (sides and base) and dust with cocoa powder.
  2. Sift the Dry Ingredients and whisk to combine in a bowl.
  3. Place butter and sugar in a bowl and beat with electric beater or in stand mixer until smooth and well combined (use paddle attachment if using stand mixer).
  4. Add eggs, one at a time, beating in between to combine. At first it will look curdle, keep beating until it is smooth.
  5. Add vegetable oil, vinegar, vanilla, buttermilk and red food colouring. Beat until combined and smooth
  6. Add Dry Ingredients. Beat until just combined, some small lumps is ok, that is better than over mixing.
  7. Divide batter between cake pans. Bake for 25 – 30 minutes on the same shelf, or until a toothpick inserted into the centre comes out clean.
  8. Rest for 10 minutes in the pan then turn out onto a cooling rack and allow to cool.
